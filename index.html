Kobe Bryant

Basketball Legend "Black Mamba".

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • August 23, 1978 - Kobe Bean Bryant was born in Philadelphia.
  • 1996 - Kobe Bryant was selected by Charlotte Hornets with the first round, 13th overall pick in NBA Draft and he was then traded to Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997 - He won the 1997 Slam Dunk contest and was named an all star in his second season.
  • 2000-2002 - He won three consecutive NBA Championship together with his teammate Shaquille O'Neal.
  • January 22, 2006 - He scored a career-high 81 points; the second most points scored in a single game in league history, behind Wilt Chamberlain's 100-point game in 1962.
  • 2008 - He was named NBA Most Valuable Player.
  • 2009-2010 - Bryant led the team to two consecutive championships and both times being named NBA Finals MVP.
  • 2015-2016 season - He retired at the age of 38.
  • January 26, 2020 - Bryant died, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others, in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California, at the age of 41.
